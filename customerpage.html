FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

You can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before the pickup time through the Bookings page or by contacting our support team. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's licence, a government-issued ID or passport, and a credit or debit card in your name. International customers may require an International Driving Permit.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Yes, all our rentals include basic insurance coverage for the entire rental period. You can also opt for premium coverage during checkout for additional peace of mind.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period subject to car availability. Contact us at least 6 hours before the return time and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns are charged at the standard daily rate pro-rated per hour. If you know you'll be late, please call us in advance so we can adjust the booking accordingly.

How does the refund process work?

Refunds for eligible cancellations are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method. You'll receive a confirmation email once the refund has been initiated.
